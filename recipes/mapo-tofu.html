Description

I think the most difficult part of this dish is nailing down that tangy mouth-piercing spice. But so long as you have enough chilli variations, you should not need those specialized spices you read on other websites! This is a recipe I somewhat improved upon from the net after tasting it myself a few times, so I hope you like it!

Ingredients

(Serving Size: 2)

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 fresh chilli peppers
  • 8 dried red chillis
  • 21/2tbsp ginger(finely minced)
  • 21/2tbsp garlic(finely minced)
  • 200ml chicken stock
  • 450g silken tofu
  • 250g minced pork
  • 3 tbsp spicy bean sauce
  • 11/2tbsp cornstarch
